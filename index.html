What I do

As an innovation specialist, I partner with organisations to help create new business models, products and services or improve those that already exist. My four focus areas are:

  • Business Design
  • Service Design
  • Capability Building

Alongside delivering great work that has measurable impact, my aim is to further help those I work with by imparting knowledge in the design thinking, lean startup and agile methodologies. When my work finishes, I want to make sure someone else can carry it on.
