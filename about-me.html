I live in the the Shropshire countryside with my family and our dog Monty (more on our namesake below).

I started my career in financial services, becoming a Chartered Accountant before I becoming a strategy and innovation specialist.

With my unique background, I excel at balancing the requirements of organisations with the unmet needs of their customers.

I combine my business acumen with strategy, design thinking and lean start-up methodologies to craft sustainable business models, centred around products and services that directly correspond to consumer needs.

Throughout my career, I've kept an eye on the strategy and innovation services offered outside of London.

Black Dog Innovation was born on a Tuesday morning, over my morning coffee, whilst staring absent-mindedly at Monty.

After painstaking research, I decided it was time to provide these valuable services irrespective of whether or not you're situated within the M25.
